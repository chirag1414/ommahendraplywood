What are WPC Louvers?

WPC (Wood-Plastic Composite) Louvers are highly durable, weather-resistant, and eco-friendly wall panels used for both interior and exterior decoration.

Specifications

  • Material: Wood-Plastic Composite (WPC)
  • Thickness: 10mm, 12mm, 15mm, 18mm
  • Size: Standard Sizes Available
  • Water & Termite Resistant: Yes
  • Applications: Interior Walls, Exterior Cladding, Ceilings
  • Available Colors: Wooden Finish, Matte.
