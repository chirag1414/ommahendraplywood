Different Types of Plywood

Here are the various types of plywood we offer, each with unique specifications and features:

Marine Plywood (Red Core)

Specification:

  • Water-resistant & Red Core
  • Ideal for House
  • Thickness: 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 18mm
  • Grade: BWR (Boiling Water Resistant)

B.W.P Plywood

Specification:

  • Used for furniture, doors, and interior applications
  • Thickness: 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 19mm
  • Grade: B.W.P (Boiling Water Proof)

Hardwood Plywood

Specification:

  • Made from hardwood species like oak, maple
  • Used for high-strength applications
  • Thickness: 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 18mm

Block Board

Specification:

  • Made from Pinewood and Hardwood
  • Used for high-strength applications silding door or main door
  • Thickness: 19mm, 25mm
  • Grade: BWP (Boiling Water Proof)

Flexible Plywood

Specification:

  • Used for curved surfaces and furniture
  • Thickness: 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 18mm
  • Flexible and easy to mold
